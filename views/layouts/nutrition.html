Top Nutrition Tips

1. Eat More Vegetables

Fill half your plate with colorful veggies to pack in fiber, vitamins, and minerals.

2. Stay Hydrated

Drink at least 2-3 liters of water daily to keep your energy and digestion on point.

3. Prioritize Protein

Support your muscles by adding lean meats, eggs, dairy, or legumes to every meal.

4. Choose Whole Grains

Swap white rice and bread for brown rice, oats, and whole grain pasta for better fiber.
