Frequently Asked Questions

What's included in the membership fee?
All memberships include access to our facilities during operating hours, locker rooms, and basic amenities. Higher tiers include additional benefits as listed.
Can I freeze my membership?
Yes, you can freeze your membership for up to 3 months per year for medical reasons or travel. A small monthly maintenance fee applies during freezes.
Do you offer student discounts?
We offer 15% discounts for students with valid ID. Please bring your ID when signing up.
What's your cancellation policy?
You can cancel anytime with 30 days notice. No long-term contracts required.
