Your Essential 15 Supplements

Discover the key supplements that can support your health journey. Each entry provides clear, concise information on recommended use and important restrictions.

Vitamins

Vitamin D3

Recommended Use: 1000-4000 IU daily with a meal containing fats for better absorption.

Restrictions: Avoid excessive doses (>10,000 IU/day) without medical supervision. May interact with certain medications.

Minerals

Magnesium

Recommended Use: 200-400 mg daily, preferably in glycinate or citrate form. Take in the evening for better sleep.

Restrictions: May cause diarrhea at high doses. Caution for those with kidney disease.

Vitamins

Vitamin C

Recommended Use: 500-1000 mg daily, especially during cold and flu season or for immune support.

Restrictions: High doses can cause digestive upset. Avoid if you have kidney stones or hemochromatosis.

Omega-3

Fish Oil (Omega-3)

Recommended Use: 1000-2000 mg of EPA+DHA daily. Take with food to reduce fishy burps.

Restrictions: May increase bleeding risk, consult doctor if on blood thinners. Can cause upset stomach.

Probiotics

Probiotics

Recommended Use: 10-50 billion CFUs daily, ideally on an empty stomach or with food as directed by product.

Restrictions: Mild digestive discomfort initially. Consult doctor if immunocompromised or critically ill.

Amino Acids

Creatine

Recommended Use: 3-5g daily, monohydrate form, with water. Can be taken any time of day.

Restrictions: Ensure adequate hydration. Caution for those with kidney issues, consult doctor.

Herbs

Ashwagandha

Recommended Use: 300-600 mg of root extract daily, often taken before bed for stress relief.

Restrictions: May interact with thyroid medications, sedatives. Avoid if pregnant or breastfeeding.

Joint Health

Glucosamine & Chondroitin

Recommended Use: 1500 mg Glucosamine, 1200 mg Chondroitin daily. Take with meals.

Restrictions: May interact with blood thinners. Not recommended for shellfish allergies if from shellfish source.

Antioxidants

CoQ10

Recommended Use: 100-200 mg daily, especially for those on statin medications. Take with food.

Restrictions: Generally well-tolerated. May interact with blood thinners and blood pressure medications.

Energy

B-Complex

Recommended Use: One capsule daily with food, preferably in the morning for energy support.

Restrictions: Generally safe. High doses of certain B vitamins may cause mild side effects.

Digestive Health

Digestive Enzymes

Recommended Use: Take with meals to aid digestion and nutrient absorption.

Restrictions: May cause digestive upset in some. Consult doctor if you have ulcers or pancreatitis.

Herbs

Turmeric (Curcumin)

Recommended Use: 500-1000 mg daily, with black pepper for enhanced absorption, for anti-inflammatory support.

Restrictions: May interact with blood thinners. Caution for those with gallstones or liver issues.

Minerals

Zinc

Recommended Use: 15-30 mg daily, typically with food to prevent nausea, for immune support.

Restrictions: High doses can lead to copper deficiency. May interact with certain antibiotics.

Amino Acids

BCAAs

Recommended Use: 5-10g before, during, or after workouts for muscle recovery.

Restrictions: Generally safe. Ensure proper balance with other amino acids for optimal benefit.

Adaptogens

Rhodiola Rosea

Recommended Use: 200-600 mg daily, typically in the morning, for stress and fatigue.

Restrictions: May cause insomnia if taken late. Consult doctor if on antidepressants or blood thinners.
