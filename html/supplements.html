Top 15 Essential Supplements

Vitamins

Vitamin D3

Recommended Use: 1000-4000 IU daily with a meal containing fats for better absorption.
Restrictions: Avoid excessive doses (>10,000 IU/day) without medical supervision. May interact with certain medications.
Minerals

Magnesium

Recommended Use: 200-400 mg daily, preferably in glycinate or citrate form. Take in the evening for better sleep.
Restrictions: May cause diarrhea at high doses. Caution for those with kidney disease.
Omega-3

Fish Oil

Recommended Use: 1000-3000 mg combined EPA/DHA daily with meals to reduce fishy aftertaste.
Restrictions: May thin blood - consult doctor before surgery or if on blood thinners.
Probiotics

Probiotic Complex

Recommended Use: 1-10 billion CFU daily, preferably on empty stomach in the morning.
Restrictions: May cause temporary bloating. Caution for immunocompromised individuals.
Herbal

Turmeric/Curcumin

Recommended Use: 500-1000 mg daily with black pepper (piperine) for enhanced absorption.
Restrictions: May interact with blood thinners. Not recommended for those with gallbladder issues.
Amino Acids

L-Theanine

Recommended Use: 100-400 mg daily, can be taken with caffeine for focused calm.
Restrictions: Generally safe but may cause drowsiness at higher doses.
Antioxidants

Coenzyme Q10

Recommended Use: 100-300 mg daily with a fatty meal for best absorption.
Restrictions: May lower blood pressure. Caution with blood pressure medications.
Minerals

Zinc

Recommended Use: 15-30 mg daily, preferably with food to prevent nausea.
Restrictions: Long-term high doses can cause copper deficiency. Avoid nasal zinc sprays.
Vitamins

Vitamin B Complex

Recommended Use: 1 capsule daily with food, preferably in the morning.
Restrictions: May cause bright yellow urine (normal). High doses of B6 can cause nerve damage.
Herbal

Ashwagandha

Recommended Use: 300-600 mg extract daily, preferably with meals.
Restrictions: Avoid if pregnant or with thyroid disorders. May interact with sedatives.
Amino Acids

Creatine Monohydrate

Recommended Use: 3-5 g daily, with or without food. Loading phase optional (20g/day for 5-7 days).
Restrictions: Ensure adequate water intake. Caution with kidney disease (though generally safe).
Electrolytes

Electrolyte Complex

Recommended Use: As needed, especially during exercise or illness. Follow package instructions.
Restrictions: Watch sodium content if hypertensive. Avoid high potassium if on certain medications.
Digestive

Digestive Enzymes

Recommended Use: With meals as needed, especially larger or fatty meals.
Restrictions: Generally safe but may cause stomach upset in some individuals.
Herbal

Ginger Root

Recommended Use: 1000 mg daily or as needed for nausea/digestive support.
Restrictions: May interact with blood thinners. Use caution with gallstones.
Collagen

Collagen Peptides

Recommended Use: 10-20 g daily, mixed in beverages or food.
Restrictions: Generally safe but may cause mild digestive upset in some.
