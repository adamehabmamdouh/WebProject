Gym Workout Splits

Optimize your training with these proven routines

Push/Pull/Legs Split

A classic 3-day rotation that groups exercises by movement patterns

Push Day (Chest, Shoulders, Triceps)

  • Flat Barbell Bench Press 4 sets × 6-8 reps
  • Incline Dumbbell Press 3 sets × 8-10 reps
  • Overhead Shoulder Press 4 sets × 6-8 reps
  • Lateral Raises 3 sets × 12-15 reps
  • Triceps Dips 3 sets × 10-12 reps

Pull Day (Back, Biceps)

  • Deadlifts 4 sets × 5 reps
  • Pull-Ups 4 sets × 8-10 reps
  • Bent Over Rows 3 sets × 8-10 reps
  • Face Pulls 3 sets × 12-15 reps
  • Barbell Curls 3 sets × 10-12 reps

Legs Day

  • Squats 4 sets × 6-8 reps
  • Romanian Deadlifts 3 sets × 8-10 reps
  • Leg Press 3 sets × 10-12 reps
  • Leg Curls 3 sets × 12-15 reps
  • Calf Raises 4 sets × 15-20 reps

Arnold Schwarzenegger Split

The legendary 6-day split used by Arnold during his bodybuilding career

Day 1: Chest & Back

  • Bench Press 5 sets × 6-10 reps
  • Incline Dumbbell Press 5 sets × 6-10 reps
  • Pull-Ups 5 sets × max reps
  • Bent Over Rows 5 sets × 6-10 reps

Day 2: Shoulders & Arms

  • Military Press 5 sets × 6-10 reps
  • Lateral Raises 5 sets × 10-15 reps
  • Barbell Curls 5 sets × 6-10 reps
  • Close-Grip Bench Press 5 sets × 6-10 reps

Day 3: Legs & Lower Back

  • Squats 5 sets × 6-10 reps
  • Leg Curls 5 sets × 10-15 reps
  • Calf Raises 5 sets × 15-20 reps
  • Deadlifts 5 sets × 6-10 reps

Day 4: Repeat Day 1

Day 5: Repeat Day 2

Day 6: Repeat Day 3

Day 7: Rest

Training Tips: Arnold typically trained each body part twice per week with high volume.
Note: This is an advanced split requiring significant recovery. Not recommended for beginners.
